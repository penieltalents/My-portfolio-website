Case Study — Jeff Dalzell Fitness

5 first-page rankings in one week — in one of the web’s most competitive niches

The fitness and personal training industry is brutally competitive in search. Established brands, high-authority domains, and well-funded blogs dominate SERPs. Getting a fitness site to rank on page one — for five separate queries — in a single week required precision, not volume.

The approach was surgical: a full technical SEO audit, aggressive on-page optimisation for high-intent keywords, structured internal linking, and targeted link building outreach to build early authority signals fast.
